Project and Systems Management

While I no longer work or consult in project management capacities, I still am happy to discuss any systems management questions you might have. Please send me an email if you are interested.

IPRO Website Redesign

Date: 2007

Description: I was the project manager for the IPRO website redesign. I managed a front-end designer and developer as she inventoried site content, updated site architecture, redesigned the site, and ported content onto a content management system.
